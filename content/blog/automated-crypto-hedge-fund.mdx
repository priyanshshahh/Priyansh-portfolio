## The honest negative

Project CHF (`v1.0-research-release`) found statistically significant candidate signals - best Rank IC 0.0275 with t-stat 7.10 - but after deterministic portfolio construction, transaction costs, and benchmark comparison against BTC, ETH, BTC/ETH 50-50, and equal-weight universes, **no strategy achieved verified alpha**.

`alpha_verified = false` is reported as a successful research outcome.

## Why this matters

A system designed to find alpha is easy to fool. A system designed to *reject* unsupported alpha claims is harder and more valuable. CHF's 9-agent pipeline enforces:

- Leakage-safe features and exact forward labels
- Purged + embargoed walk-forward screening
- Deterministic allocation (no research-stage alpha claims)
- Cost-aware backtesting against four benchmarks

<Callout title="Strongest backtest" variant="architecture">
linear_ridge / market_only / 30d achieved 147.36% total return and 0.7521 Sharpe - but still lost to BTC (305.50%) and BTC/ETH 50-50 (178.04%). Alpha verification failed.
</Callout>

## Takeaway

The pipeline did exactly what a trustworthy alpha-research process should do: surface candidates, test them honestly, and reject claims that don't clear the benchmark bar.
