## Executive Summary

Athlix AI is a Next.js interactive web application that models long-term wealth trajectories for professional athletes, accounting for contract structures, investment returns, tax implications, and lifestyle spending scenarios.

## Problem & Constraints

Professional athletes face unique financial planning challenges — compressed earning windows, volatile income, and complex contract structures requiring accessible simulation tools.

## Methodology

- Built interactive Next.js frontend with scenario sliders and visualization
- Modeled wealth trajectories with Monte Carlo and deterministic projections
- Incorporated contract structures, tax brackets, and investment return assumptions
- Designed comparative scenario analysis for spending and investment strategies
