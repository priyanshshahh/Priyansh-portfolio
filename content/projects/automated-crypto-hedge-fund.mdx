## Executive Summary

CHF+ X is an autonomous crypto hedge fund framework combining gradient boosting and deep learning forecasts with hierarchical risk parity allocation, orchestrated through containerized scheduling infrastructure.

## Problem & Constraints

Crypto markets demand multi-horizon forecasting across a Top-100 asset universe while accounting for feature decay, realistic trading frictions, and dynamic risk allocation under volatile regime shifts.

<Callout title="Architecture" variant="architecture">
APScheduler ingests market and on-chain data → Feature engineering with decay tracking → XGBoost + LSTM ensemble → HRP/Top-K allocation → vectorbt backtesting engine.
</Callout>

## Methodology

- Engineered walk-forward validation with Optuna hyperparameter tuning
- Implemented dynamic Feature Decay tracking to down-weight declining signals
- Deployed Hierarchical Risk Parity and Top-K allocation within vectorbt
- Containerized pipeline with Docker and APScheduler for autonomous execution
