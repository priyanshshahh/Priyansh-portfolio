## Executive Summary

An autonomous AI agent that monitors real-time news sentiment and CoinMarketCap analytics to identify mispriced prediction market opportunities on Polymarket and execute trades accordingly.

## Problem & Constraints

Prediction markets require rapid synthesis of news events, sentiment signals, and market pricing — with strict risk controls and execution latency requirements.

<Callout title="Architecture" variant="architecture">
News ingestion → sentiment scoring → CMC market context → opportunity detection → risk checks → Polymarket execution.
</Callout>

## Methodology

- Built real-time news ingestion and sentiment analysis pipeline
- Integrated CoinMarketCap analytics for cross-market context
- Designed opportunity scoring model for prediction market mispricing
- Implemented autonomous execution with position sizing and risk limits
