## Executive Summary

Genzzz is a RAG-powered chatbot trained on YouTube creator transcripts, enabling conversational access to growth strategies, content insights, and channel analytics through LangChain, FAISS vector search, and Gemini 2.5 Flash.

## Problem & Constraints

YouTube growth knowledge is scattered across hours of video content. The system must retrieve relevant transcript segments and generate accurate, context-aware responses efficiently.

<Callout title="Architecture" variant="architecture">
Transcript ingestion → chunking + embedding → FAISS vector store → LangChain retrieval chain → Gemini 2.5 Flash generation.
</Callout>

## Methodology

- Extracted and processed YouTube transcripts into searchable chunks
- Built FAISS vector index for semantic retrieval
- Orchestrated RAG pipeline with LangChain retrieval chains
- Deployed Gemini 2.5 Flash for low-latency response generation
